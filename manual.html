Welcome!

Meet the G4 Compiler, a tool for diving into the world of Automata!. Pick between two regex options, each with its own DFA, and get hands-on with simulating their transitions. But it doesn't stop there! You also get the corresponding context-free grammars (CFG) and pushdown automata (PDA) for a deeper dive into computational theory.

Instructions

To get started on using G4 Compiler, here are the instructions that can help make your experience with the compiler a little bit easier.


Step 1:

Choose from the 2 RegEx available in the dropdown menu.

Step 2:

Click the "Show DFA" button to generate the Definite Finite Automata diagram.

Step 3:

Input a string so that you can start observing how the transitions will occur in the diagram.

Step 4:

Press the "Start" button to begin the simulation!

Step 5:

Watch the compiler show how the transitions work!.

Step 6:

In the end, it will show you if your string is "Valid" or "Invalid".

Step 7:

Check out the bottom part of the page to see the 2 RegEx's corresponding CFG and PDA!
